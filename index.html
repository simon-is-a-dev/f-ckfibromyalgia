What is it?

It's not just "in your head". It's real, it's painful, and it's time we talked about it properly.

Definition

Fibromyalgia is better described as a "central sensitivity syndrome". It's a long-term condition that causes pain all over the body. But it's more than just pain.

Reality

It's the brain amplifying pain signals. It's waking up tired after 10 hours of sleep. It's the "fibro fog" that makes simple thoughts slip away like smoke.

The Story

"You don't look sick."

I heard that a lot. When I started my journey with chronic pain, I felt isolated. The medical system felt like a maze with no exit. I tracked my symptoms on scraps of paper, trying to find patterns in the chaos.

That's why I built Frogsy. I needed a tool to make sense of the pain, to visualize the invisible battles we fight every day. And now, I want to share what I've learned with the world.

This project is about validation. It's about data. It's about saying F*CK Fibromyalgia and taking back control.
