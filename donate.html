The Fibromyalgia Project supports The American Fibromyalgia Syndrome Association (AFSA).

Why AFSA?

AFSA is the only nonprofit organization dedicated solely to funding research into fibromyalgia.

Unlike other organizations that focus on "awareness" or "coping", AFSA focuses on finding the causes and treatments for this condition.

They are all-volunteer, meaning more of your money goes directly to science, not salaries.
